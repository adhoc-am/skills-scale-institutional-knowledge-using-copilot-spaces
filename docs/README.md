# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to OctoAcme's project management process documentation. This guide serves as a central landing page to help you navigate our project management practices and find the information you need.

## Overview

**OctoAcme Project Management Processes – Overview**

OctoAcme's approach prioritizes customer value, iterative delivery, and transparent ownership. Projects start with validated business need and stakeholder alignment, structured in a Project One-pager. Core roles—Project Manager, Product Manager, Developer, and QA—work through a lifecycle of initiation, planning, execution, release, and retrospective, ensuring measurable outcomes, defined responsibilities, and continuous improvement. Communication is intentional, incorporating regular standups, stakeholder updates, and demos. Quality assurance leverages automated and manual testing, CI/CD, and clear acceptance criteria.

### Project Lifecycle Documents

The following documents walk through each phase of the project lifecycle:

#### [Project Initiation](octoacme-project-initiation.md)

Understand how to validate and authorize new work. This guide covers creating a Project One-pager, aligning stakeholders, defining success criteria, and making go/no-go decisions before moving into planning.

#### [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)

Learn how to turn approved initiatives into actionable plans. This document covers backlog creation, estimation, defining the Definition of Done, managing dependencies, and creating release timelines.

#### [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md)

Guidance for day-to-day project execution and progress tracking. Covers team rhythm (standups, demos), workflow management using project boards, quality and testing practices, reporting metrics, and blocker escalation procedures.

#### [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md)

Learn how to identify, manage, and communicate risks and dependencies. Includes guidance on maintaining a risk register, stakeholder communication templates, and escalation paths for issues requiring management attention.

#### [Release & Deployment](octoacme-release-and-deployment.md)

Standardized practices for releasing features to production. Covers release types (patch, minor, major), pre-release requirements, deployment checklists, rollback procedures, and release note templates.

#### [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md)

Guidance on capturing learnings and converting them into actionable improvements. Learn how to run effective retrospectives after sprints, releases, or incidents, and track improvement actions to foster a culture of continuous improvement.

## Getting Started

1. **New to OctoAcme?** Start with the [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md) to understand our approach and principles.

2. **Starting a new project?** Follow the project lifecycle in order: [Initiation](octoacme-project-initiation.md) → [Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md) → [Execution](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) → [Release](octoacme-release-and-deployment.md) → [Retrospective](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md).

3. **Need to understand a specific role?** Check the [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) document.

4. **Managing risks or stakeholder communication?** See [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md).
