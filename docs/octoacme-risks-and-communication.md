# OctoAcme — Risk Management & Communication

## Purpose
Explain how to identify, manage, and communicate risks and dependencies.

## Risk Register
The **Risk Owner** maintains a simple table with:
- ID
- Description
- Impact (High/Med/Low)
- Likelihood (High/Med/Low)
- Owner (individual responsible for mitigation)
- Mitigation plan
- Status

## Risk Lifecycle
- **Identify**: During planning and ongoing execution (all team members contribute, Risk Owner coordinates)
- **Assess**: Risk Owner estimates impact and likelihood with input from technical and product teams
- **Mitigate**: Reduced via actions and contingency plans; Risk Owner coordinates with mitigation owners
- **Monitor**: Risk Owner reviews at weekly syncs and updates status
- **Escalate**: Risk Owner escalates major risks to Steering Group via Stakeholder Liaison when needed

## Risk Ownership & Accountability
- The **Risk Owner** role has overall accountability for risk management process
- Individual risks may be assigned to specific team members for mitigation execution
- The **Project Manager** partners with Risk Owner to integrate risk mitigation into project plans
- The **Stakeholder Liaison** helps communicate risk status to external stakeholders

## Stakeholder Communication
The **Stakeholder Liaison** coordinates external communications and ensures alignment:
- Identify stakeholder groups and communication needs (e.g., engineering, sales, support, executives)
- Provide regular updates (weekly or milestone-based) tailored to stakeholder needs
- Use a single source of truth (project README or release doc) for status
- Gather stakeholder feedback and ensure concerns are routed to appropriate team members
- Partner with **Project Manager** on messaging and timing of communications
- Coordinate with **Release Readiness Lead** during release cycles for stakeholder readiness
